Bibliographic Theory, Library Systems, & The Book

In the classical book publishing system, our patron first used the library's card catalog and moved to early (and some libraries even still do) early computer catalogs from dedicated terminals, to our current era of commerical search engines, Library Services Platforms, Discovery Services, and webscale products.

How will library systems handle E-Books? Marshell Breeding predicts that in the next year or so he "anticipate significant progress in online catalog and discovery products. Ebook integration has advanced into the must-have category of features for any online catalog or discovery product oriented to public libraries. The ability to implement this feature depends on the availability of APIs exposed by the ebook lending services." from Library Technology Forecast for 2014 and Beyond
